## My Story
I started programming in 2010, inspired by watching *The Social Network* and realizing I could do more than just be a follower of technological news. Under the name *[doctortino](https://instagram.com/doctortino)*, I've launched several projects using the LAMP stack, primarily as a means of learning and artistic expression, while not giving the necessary attention to marketability.

In 2017, I faced a significant personal loss when my father, who had supported me emotionally and financed my projects, [passed away](https://martinmorando.com/mucho-mas-que-un-padre). To change my prospects, I began a BBA in 2018, but, two subjects away from graduating, I'm finding out I did not acquire the market skills I desired. In 2019, eager to try low-budget projects, I started *[criptowiki](https://criptowiki.com)*, anticipating a growing demand for cryptocurrency. However, the following year and before the crypto winter, [I shifted my focus to Bitcoin](https://github.com/criptowiki/criptowiki/issues/2) due to its [technical](https://martinmorando.medium.com/good-observation-67f8c4b8d1e0) and [economic superiority](https://martinmorando.medium.com/when-one-starts-exploring-cryptocurrencies-it-happens-that-one-may-be-tempted-by-altcoins-and-3b61369a2a97). In 2023, I launched *[BitcoinArgy](https://bitcoinargy.com)*, an efficient bitcoin news aggregator focused on Argentina, to keep up with related events while avoiding the biases of social networks algorithms.

Over the past two years, I've realized that my low profile has backfired, as recruiters often overlook my potential and imply that I haven't engaged in any kind of work. I'm also realizing that I know much more than I thought, but I felt less assured due to not having a formal degree, especially after observing that some degree accumulators may not have the practical skills I anticipated.

Now, I'm opening up more to the world while systematically learning and experimenting. I am determined to keep pushing forward until the spark ignites.

*"The truth will set you free."*
